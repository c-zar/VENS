The Sekushi House is the first ever all-female anime style avatar PFP collection releasing on the Ethereum, Solana, and Terra blockchains. The collection consists of 6969 uniquely generated digital collectibles of various rarity. Every model consists of up to 12 traits chosen from a set of hundreds attributes. With all the varaition in the collection, there are billions of possible combinations.

Sekushi means “sexy” in Japanese. Sexy certainly holds the meanings of “sexually attractive or exciting” and “sexually aroused”, but it also is commonly used as a highly positive comparison in terms of the standards of other human activity. We aim to redefine the word. Sexy can be a metaphor for appealing, exciting, desirable, attractive, educated, wise, fair, interesting and stimulating- which is the goal of our collection.
