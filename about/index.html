About Us

We are a small team of entrepenuers related by blood. We believe that the digital space is a critical tool for increasing transparency and integrity in the art market. Our team consisting of artists, developers, and creative directors collaborate with under-represented artists in order to expose their art as NFTs on the blockchain. We are focused on partnering with artist who come from 3rd world countries, connecting their art with our developers and in house artist to release amazing collections onto the blockchain. By showcasing their art around the world, we also hope to inspire art lovers and collectors alike to explore new artistic horizons, to embrace new cultures and to be inspired by the works of talented artists from unrepresented regions
