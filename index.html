The NFT market, like many tech-centric areas, has traditionally been dominated by men. While the rise of blockchain-based digital artwork is notable, the majority of the artists responsible for creating today’s most sought after NFTs are men. The ratio of male to female NFT artists remains unclear, but statistics show that there are considerably less women involved in the overall crypto sector: A recent survey conducted by cryptocurrency exchange Gemini found that just 26% of females hold crypto. We are a proud Woman-led NFT Studio with a focus on collaborations with female artist. Our goal is to increase female engagement in crypto.

“Women belong in all places where decisions are being made. It shouldn’t be that women are the exception” — Ruth Bader-Ginsburg.

The Sekushi House is the first ever all-female anime style avatar PFP collection releasing on the Ethereum, Solana, and Terra blockchains. The collection consists of 6969 uniquely generated digital collectibles of various rarity. Every collictible consists of up to 12 traits chosen from a set of hundreds attributes. With all the varaition in the collection, there are billions of possible combinations.

Sekushi means “sexy” in Japanese. Sexy certainly holds the meanings of “sexually attractive or exciting” and “sexually aroused”, but it also is commonly used as a highly positive comparison in terms of the standards of other human activity. We aim to redefine the word. Sexy can be a metaphor for appealing, exciting, desirable, attractive, educated, wise, fair, interesting and stimulating- which is the goal of our collection.
