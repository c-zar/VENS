Projects

The NFT market, like many tech-centric areas, has traditionally been dominated by men, and a women-led project was a much-welcomed change. While the rise of blockchain-based digital artwork is notable, many of the artists responsible for creating today’s most sought after NFTs are men. While the ratio of male to female NFT artists remains unclear, statistics show that there are considerably less women than men involved in the overall crypto sector. A recent survey conducted by cryptocurrency exchange Gemini found that just 26% of females hold crypto. We are a proud Female-led NFT Studio. With out team consisting of 80% women in the field of Technology, Fine Arts and Management. Our goal is to increase female engagement in crypto

“Women belong in all places where decisions are being made. It shouldn’t be that women are the exception” — Ruth Bader-Ginsburg.
