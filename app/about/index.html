About Us

We believe that the digital space is a critical tool for increasing transparency and integrity in the art market. Our team of artists, developers, and creative directors collaborate with under-represented artists in order to release their NFT art on the blockchain. Our team is working around the clock, committing their energy to promoting Ven Studio Collections in different platform to maximize traffic and increase audience. Our artist come from 3rd world countries and we connect them with our software engineers and in house artist to release amazing art onto the blockchain. In showcasing Their Collections around the world, we also hope to allow art lovers and collectors alike to explore new artistic horizons, to embrace new cultures and to be inspired by the works of talented artists that they never would have come across if it wasn't for Ven Studio.
